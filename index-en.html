Canadian Risk Factor Atlas

The Canadian Risk Factor Atlas is an interactive mapping tool that presents prevalence estimates on key chronic disease and mental health risk factors at the following geographic levels: Province and territories, Health Regions, and by Census Metropolitan Areas (CMAs) and large Census Agglomerations (CAs). For each indicator, estimates are disaggregated by four socio-demographic levels: geography, youth or adults, sex, population centre/rural area and key socioeconomic determinants.

Navigate the map by hovering over each area. Click an area to lock it and zoom in and click again to zoom out.
