Canadian Risk Factor Atlas

The Canadian Risk Factor Atlas is an interactive tool that presents prevalence estimates on key disease risk factors at several geographic levels (e.g. National, by Province and Territories, Health Regions, Census Metropolitan areas and more). For each indicator, estimates are disaggregated by four levels (i.e. by geography, by youth and adults, by sex and by key socioeconomic determinants).
